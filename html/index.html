One Photo. One Like. One Year.

Once a Year is a website designed around the idea that photography is beautiful when shared. Too often however, we get caught up in the act of sharing things, or involving others within our processes.

Our website itself is simple, as the photography should be the focus here. Each user may upload one photo, and like one other photo, as the beauty of photography is not derrived from its abundance.

Every year, as we roll into the new year, all uploaded photographs will be removed from our website. This will be accompanied with the preservation of a number of most-liked photographs, which will be maintained on our wall of photography.
